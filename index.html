About me

I am a PhD student in control and model reduction, with a focus on infinite-dimensional systems and PDEs. I work on balanced truncation, finite elements, and large-scale exponential integrators.

What I share on GitHub

  • Code for numerical simulation of PDEs
  • Model reduction scripts for DAEs
  • Data from published research
