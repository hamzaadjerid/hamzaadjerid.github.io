About me

I am a Ph.D. student in Applied Mathematics at Virginia Tech, with a prior Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. My research focuses on control theory, model reduction, numerical analysis, and numerical linear algebra. I am currently working on developing polynomial approximations for nonlinear controllers in nonlinear dynamical systems.
